### Unit Converters
The most popular types of unit converters focus on everyday measurements and conversions. Here's a list of the top ones:

- **Length/Distance**: Inches to centimeters, feet to meters.
- **Weight/Mass**: Pounds to kilograms, ounces to grams.
- **Temperature**: Celsius to Fahrenheit, Kelvin to Celsius.
- **Volume**: Liters to gallons, milliliters to ounces.
- **Currency**: USD to EUR, BTC to USD (includes crypto).
- **Time**: Hours to seconds, UTC to local time zones.
- **Speed**: MPH to km/h, knots to m/s.
- **Area**: Square feet to square meters, acres to hectares.

### File Converters
File converters are widely used for compatibility across devices and software. Popular types include:

- **Document Formats**: PDF to Word (DOCX), Word to PDF.
- **Image Formats**: JPG to PNG, PNG to GIF.
- **Video Formats**: MP4 to AVI, MOV to MP4.
- **Audio Formats**: MP3 to WAV, FLAC to MP3.
- **Archive/Compression**: ZIP to RAR, TAR to ZIP.
- **E-book Formats**: EPUB to PDF, MOBI to EPUB.
- **Spreadsheet Formats**: XLSX to CSV, CSV to Google Sheets.
- **Presentation Formats**: PPTX to PDF, PDF to PPTX.

### Media Converters
Media converters primarily handle audio and video transformations for playback or editing. Common types are:

- **Video Codecs**: H.264 to H.265 (HEVC), MPEG-2 to MP4.
- **Video Containers**: AVI to MP4, MKV to WebM.
- **Audio Codecs**: AAC to MP3, Opus to FLAC.
- **Audio Containers**: WAV to OGG, M4A to MP3.
- **Resolution/Quality**: 1080p to 4K upscaling, 4K to 720p downscaling.
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps to 60fps, NTSC to PAL (for broadcast).
- **Subtitle Integration**: SRT to embedded subtitles in MP4.
- **Batch Media**: Multiple files from one format to another (e.g., DVD to digital).

### Calculators
Calculators range from basic arithmetic to specialized tools. The most popular types are:

- **Basic Arithmetic**: Addition, subtraction, multiplication, division.
- **Scientific**: Trigonometric functions (sin, cos), logarithms, exponents.
- **Financial**: Loan interest, compound interest, ROI (return on investment).
- **Mortgage/Loan**: Monthly payments, amortization schedules.
- **BMI/Health**: Body Mass Index, calorie counters.
- **Tip/Tax**: Restaurant tips, sales tax calculations.
- **Date/Time**: Age calculator, days between dates.
- **Statistics**: Mean, median, standard deviation.

### Filters
In the context of digital tools (e.g., photo editing, data processing, or audio), filters enhance or modify content. Popular types include:

- **Photo/Image Filters**: Grayscale, sepia tone, vintage (e.g., Instagram-style).
- **Blur/Sharpen**: Gaussian blur, unsharp mask for focus.
- **Color Adjustment**: Brightness/contrast, hue/saturation, color balance.
- **Noise Reduction**: Denoise for photos, pop filter for audio.
- **Edge Detection**: Sobel filter for outlines in images.
- **Data Filters**: Low-pass (smooths data), high-pass (removes low frequencies).
- **Audio Filters**: Equalizer (EQ), reverb/echo effects.
- **Social Media Filters**: AR face filters, beauty enhancements (e.g., Snapchat).
